### 必读事项

To prevent unwanted updates, we will disable an option made by Sony which automatically download the update file from their servers on your vita.
### 你需要准备点啥？

* 将你的 PS Vita (TV) 连接上网络

### 操作步骤

#### 第一步 – 禁用自动下载选项


1. 移动至 `系统(System)` -> `自动启动设置(Auto-Start Settings)`
1. 取消 “下载系统软件的更新文件(Download Update File for System Software)”
1. 返回到设置的主菜单
